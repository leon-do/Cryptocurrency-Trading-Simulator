Instructions

Once you register and log in, we'll go ahead and get you started out with $100,000 that you can choose to allocate as you wish. To make a trade, you'll have to first select how you are going to pay for that trade from the 'From' drop-down list. This drop-down list will only populate with assets you have in your wallet. Next select with coin you want to buy from the 'To' drop-down list. Finally, you'll have to enter an amount for how much you wish to purchase. When you are ready to execute your trade, click the 'Transfer' button and your trade will be made based upon real time market rates. You can check the present market values for all coins available on the exchange from the table below the transfer deck.
